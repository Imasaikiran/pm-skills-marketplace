# First 90 Days as a Product Manager

## Description
Structured approach to succeeding in your first three months as a new PM, building relationships, understanding context, and delivering early wins.

## The 90-Day Framework

### Days 1-30: Learn
**Goal:** Understand the landscape

### Days 31-60: Contribute  
**Goal:** Make meaningful contributions

### Days 61-90: Lead
**Goal:** Drive initiatives independently

## Week 1: Onboarding

### Day 1
- [ ] Meet your manager
- [ ] Set up accounts and tools
- [ ] Review onboarding materials
- [ ] Understand team structure
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with team

### Days 2-5
- [ ] Deep dive into product (use it extensively)
- [ ] Review product strategy docs
- [ ] Understand metrics and dashboards
- [ ] Read past product specs
- [ ] Study customer feedback
- [ ] Review roadmap

## Month 1: Learning Mode

### Learn the Product
- [ ] Use product daily (power user level)
- [ ] Test edge cases and error states
- [ ] Review analytics dashboards
- [ ] Read support tickets
- [ ] Watch user research sessions
- [ ] Understand technical architecture

### Learn the Market
- [ ] Research competitors
- [ ] Read industry reports
- [ ] Review customer reviews
- [ ] Understand buyer personas
- [ ] Study market trends

### Learn the Business
- [ ] Business model deep dive
- [ ] Revenue drivers
- [ ] Unit economics
- [ ] Strategic priorities
- [ ] OKRs and goals

### Build Relationships
Meet with:
- [ ] Engineering leads (all)
- [ ] Designers
- [ ] Data analysts
- [ ] Sales team
- [ ] Customer success
- [ ] Marketing
- [ ] Support team
- [ ] Key stakeholders

**Questions to Ask:**
- What's working well?
- What's frustrating?
- What should I know?
- How can I help?
- What should I prioritize learning?

## Month 2: Contributing

### Quick Wins
Identify and deliver:
- Low-effort, high-impact improvements
- Long-standing small annoyances
- Data/insights projects
- Documentation gaps
- Process improvements

**Examples:**
- Clean up confusing UI copy
- Fix obvious UX issues
- Improve internal documentation
- Optimize an underperforming flow
- Add missing analytics events

### Own Your First Feature
- [ ] Lead sprint planning
- [ ] Write specs
- [ ] Work with design
- [ ] Define success metrics
- [ ] Plan rollout
- [ ] Ship something small

### Establish Communication Rhythms
- [ ] Regular stakeholder updates
- [ ] Team standups/syncs
- [ ] One-on-ones with key partners
- [ ] Product updates to leadership

## Month 3: Leading

### Drive Strategy
- [ ] Propose roadmap items
- [ ] Lead prioritization discussions
- [ ] Present vision/strategy
- [ ] Make trade-off decisions

### Own Outcomes
- [ ] Track metrics proactively
- [ ] Report on success/failures
- [ ] Iterate based on data
- [ ] Communicate impact

### Expand Influence
- [ ] Cross-functional initiatives
- [ ] Mentor junior team members
- [ ] Shape product culture
- [ ] Build external relationships

## Success Metrics

### 30-Day Goals
✅ Understand product thoroughly
✅ Meet all key stakeholders
✅ Identify quick win opportunities
✅ Build trust with eng/design teams

### 60-Day Goals
✅ Ship first feature/improvement
✅ Run first sprint planning
✅ Contribute to roadmap discussion
✅ Present insights to team

### 90-Day Goals
✅ Own a product area
✅ Make independent decisions
✅ Drive initiatives end-to-end
✅ Establish credibility

## Common Pitfalls

### ❌ Changing Everything Immediately
- Listen first, change later
- Understand why things are the way they are
- Build trust before pushing change

### ❌ Not Building Relationships
- Relationships = influence
- Invest time in 1:1s
- Learn how people prefer to work

### ❌ Staying in Learning Mode Too Long
- Balance learning with doing
- Start contributing by week 3-4
- Don't wait for perfect knowledge

### ❌ Trying to Prove Yourself Too Fast
- No need for hero projects
- Small wins build credibility
- Consistency > big splash

## Questions to Answer

### About Users
- Who are our users?
- What do they love/hate?
- What are their biggest pain points?
- How do they use the product?

### About Product
- What's the vision?
- What's the strategy?
- What's working/not working?
- What are the biggest opportunities?

### About Team
- How do we make decisions?
- What's the team culture?
- What are current pain points?
- Who are the key influencers?

### About Success
- How is success measured?
- What are we optimizing for?
- What are the goals (OKRs)?
- What would make this quarter successful?
